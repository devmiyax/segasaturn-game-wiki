## Chapter 1: Game Overview

AZEL — Panzer Dragoon RPG (released as "Panzer Dragoon Saga" outside Japan) is the third entry in Sega's Panzer Dragoon series and the only RPG in the franchise. While its predecessors were on-rails shooters, this title transplants the series' signature atmosphere and dragon-riding gameplay into a fully fledged role-playing game.

Players assume the role of Edge, a young mercenary who bonds with a mysterious dragon and embarks on a journey of vengeance and discovery across a post-apocalyptic world shaped by the ruins of an ancient civilization. The game features a unique real-time battle system built around positioning, gauge management, and dragon morphing, combined with an epic storyline and stunning 3D graphics that pushed the Sega Saturn hardware to its absolute limits.

Released near the end of the Saturn's lifespan, the game had a limited print run and is now considered one of the rarest and most sought-after RPGs ever made.

## Chapter 2: Characters

### Main Characters

**Edge**
The protagonist. A young mercenary working as a guard at an Imperial excavation site. When Craymen's rebel forces attack the site and murder his companions, Edge falls into the depths of the ruins and encounters a mysterious dragon. Driven by a desire for revenge, he sets out on a journey that will change the fate of the world.

**Azel**
A mysterious girl discovered sealed inside an ancient device at the excavation site. She is a "Drone" — an artificial being created by the lost Ancient civilization — and possesses extraordinary abilities. Azel is the key to Sestren, the vast network that controls all of the Ancients' towers and ruins. She is awakened by Craymen and initially serves as his weapon, but her encounters with Edge gradually change her perspective.

**K.F. Craymen**
Commander of the Imperial Black Fleet and a former high-ranking member of the Imperial Academy. Craymen defects from the Empire, raids the excavation site to obtain Azel, and kills Edge's companions — making him Edge's sworn enemy. However, his true motivations are far more complex than simple ambition, and his actions prove central to the story's deepest themes.

**Gash**
The leader of the Seekers, a group dedicated to uncovering the secrets of the Ancient civilization. Gash serves as Edge's most reliable ally, providing guidance, information, and support at critical moments throughout the adventure.

**Paet**
A brilliant engineer living in the Holy District of the Village of Zoah. Paet has extensive knowledge of Ancient technology and is capable of building and repairing airships and other devices. He assists Edge on multiple occasions and plays a vital role in the later stages of the story.

### Supporting Characters

**Zadoc the Compiler** — An elderly member of the Seekers who serves as the group's record-keeper.
**Arwen** — A fellow mercenary and friend of Edge at the excavation site.
**Zastava** — Craymen's ruthless subordinate and a feared Imperial officer.
**An'jou** — The leader of a caravan (a nomadic trading convoy) that Edge encounters.
**Jared** — A shopkeeper in the Village of Zoah.
**Abner** — One of Zoah's gatekeepers.

## Chapter 3: Battle System Guide

### Core Mechanics

Battles in Panzer Dragoon Saga use a real-time command system. Three **Action Gauges** are displayed at the bottom of the screen and charge over time. Each full gauge allows one action. The charging speed depends on the dragon's Agility stat — a higher Agility means more actions per unit of time.

### Attack Types

**Shot (Gun)** — Edge fires his handgun at the enemy. Most effective from the front position. Deals concentrated damage to a single target.

**Homing Laser** — The dragon's lock-on laser attack. You can lock onto multiple enemies or multiple parts of a single enemy. Most effective from the sides. The number of lock-ons increases with the dragon's Attack stat.

**Berserk** — The equivalent of magic spells in a traditional RPG. Berserks consume Berserk Points (BP) and sometimes require multiple Action Gauges. Effects include healing, stat buffs, debuffs, and powerful attacks. Berserk effectiveness scales with the dragon's Spiritual stat.

### Positioning

The battlefield is a 360-degree field centered on the enemy. You can move between four positions: Front, Rear, Left, and Right. Each enemy has **Safe Zones** (green on the radar) and **Danger Zones** (red on the radar). Staying in green zones reduces the damage you take, while red zones leave you vulnerable to devastating attacks. Mastering positioning is the single most important skill in the game.

### Dragon Morphing System

The dragon has four attributes — **Attack**, **Defense**, **Agility**, and **Spiritual** — that you can freely adjust at any time. Depending on the balance of these attributes, the dragon falls into one of five classes, each with a unique Full-Gauge ability (costs 3 gauges, 0 BP) and a passive effect when all gauges are charged:

**Normal Class** — All attributes roughly balanced (all below 150). Full-Gauge ability: **Healing Wing** (large HP recovery). Passive: slow HP regeneration.

**Attack Class** — High Attack attribute. Lock-on lasers deal more damage and gain additional lock-ons. Trade-off: reduced Spiritual power weakens Berserks. Full-Gauge ability: **Assault Wing**.

**Defense Class** — High Defense attribute. Reduces all incoming damage. Trade-off: low Agility slows gauge charging. Full-Gauge ability: **Protection Wing** (further damage reduction for the rest of the battle).

**Spiritual Class** — High Spiritual attribute. Berserks are more powerful and cost less BP. Full-Gauge ability: **Berserker Wing** (large BP recovery).

**Agility Class** — High Agility attribute. Faster gauge charging and quicker position changes. Full-Gauge ability: **Swift Wing** (temporarily increases gauge charge speed). Passive: cures all status ailments.

## Chapter 4: Step-by-Step Walkthrough

---

### DISC 1

---

#### Stage 1: Excavation Site — Lower Levels (Opening)

The story begins at Excavation Site #4, an Ancient ruin under Imperial control.

1. Watch the opening events. Edge is on guard duty at the excavation site.
2. Gunfire erupts at the entrance — Craymen's rebel forces are attacking.
3. In the ensuing cutscene, Edge's companions are killed one by one, and Edge himself is thrown off a cliff into the ruins below.
4. After regaining consciousness, Edge finds **two containers** nearby.
   - Container 1: **Shell Plate** (armor) — equip this immediately.
   - Container 2: **Handgun** and **Elevator Key** — your first weapon and a key item.
5. Use the Elevator Key to activate the elevator and proceed deeper into the ruins.
6. At the deepest level of the ruins, Edge encounters a mysterious dragon and bonds with it.

**Tip:** Do not miss the containers. Your starting equipment is precious and these items will carry you through the early battles.

---

#### Stage 2: Upper Excavation Site — Valley (Dragon Tutorial)

Immediately after mounting the dragon, you enter a tutorial section.

1. Practice the dragon's basic controls: movement, turning, ascending, and descending.
2. Fly out of the excavation site and enter the Valley area.
3. Random encounters with monsters begin here.
4. Learn the battle basics: Shot, Homing Laser, and Positioning. Pay attention to the radar display showing safe and danger zones.

**Tip:** Get into the habit of moving to safe zones (green) and avoiding danger zones (red) from the very start. This will save you throughout the entire game.

---

#### Stage 3: Canyon Deep Gulch

1. Proceed deeper into the canyon. Explore every branching path to find items.
2. At the first waterfall, you encounter the **mini-boss: Baldor Queen** — a large insect-type monster.
   - Stay on its flanks and use Homing Laser repeatedly. Avoid the front where its attacks are strongest.
3. Check behind the waterfall — there is a hidden passage with **three pyramid-shaped targets**. Shooting these unlocks secrets.
4. Continue deeper until you reach the **Wind Net** area.
5. **Wind Net Solution:** Move in the opposite direction of the fans (blowers). Past the save point, descend into the tunnel and activate the device at the dead end to stop the fans.

---

#### Stage 4: Boss — Arachnoth

A giant spider-type monster awaits at the deepest part of the canyon.

**Phase 1:**
1. Move to Arachnoth's side position.
2. When Arachnoth turns to face you, move to the opposite side.
3. Keep firing Homing Lasers while circling.
4. Repeat this cat-and-mouse pattern. After taking enough damage, Arachnoth enters "Enraged" mode.

**Phase 2:**
1. Move to the center position.
2. Arachnoth charges forward and crashes into the wall, stunning itself.
3. Unleash everything you have during the stun window — Shots, Lasers, and Berserks.
4. Repeat until defeated.

**Tip:** If all three gauges are full, use a Full-Gauge ability for massive damage during the stun.

---

### Late DISC 1 — DISC 2

---

#### Stage 5: Garil Desert

1. After leaving the canyon, you emerge into a vast open desert.
2. This is a free-roam area — explore in all directions.
3. On the northern side of the desert, locate **three stone arches**. Fly through them in sequence to trigger a giant worm that reveals a secret passage.
4. Enter the **Worm Lair** and collect items inside.
5. Discover the **Green Oasis** at the far edge of the desert.

**Tip:** The desert is large and easy to get lost in. Use landmarks (rock formations, ruin fragments) to orient yourself.

---

#### Stage 6: Village of Zoah

Beyond the desert lies the Village of Zoah, a settlement of survivors. This is a combat-free exploration and story segment.

**Checklist of important actions:**

1. **Shop near the entrance** — Talk to the shopkeeper, Jared. Examine the bookshelves to obtain the **Ship Report**, **Bible 2**, **Dragon Book 1**, and **Zoah Bible**.
2. **Gatekeeper's house (daytime)** — Visit and talk to the overweight gatekeeper Abner repeatedly. He will eventually ask to borrow **100 Dynes**. Lend the money — this investment pays off later.
3. **Bar (nighttime)** — Talk to the bartender, pay **20 Dynes**, and answer "Yes." Ask about the Empire to receive the **Picture Board**.
4. Talk to every villager to gather information and lore.
5. Visit Paet's house in the Holy District for key information about Ancient technology.

**Tip:** Zoah changes between day and night — different NPCs appear and conversations change depending on the time. Explore during both time periods.

---

#### Stage 7: Georgius — The Ancient Ship Shellcoof

1. Depart from Zoah and head to the Georgius sea region.
2. Near the whirlpool, destroy all **Guardians** (escort mechs). Do not get too close to the whirlpool itself — the water current causes damage, and Hellions may ambush you.
3. Once all Guardians are defeated, the **Ancient Ship Shellcoof** rises from the sea floor.

**Mini-boss: Gunship**
- A relatively easy fight. Position yourself at the front and trade shots with its Tracers. It falls quickly.

**Boss: Guardian Dragon**
1. When its armor plate is closed, move to a side position and build up your gauges.
2. After taking some damage, the plate opens — this is your window.
3. Move to the rear and hit it hard with Shots (gun).
4. When it enters Berserk mode, stay at the rear and keep shooting until it goes down.

---

#### Stage 8: Inside the Shellcoof

1. To enter the Shellcoof, you must activate the **pyramid-shaped relics** in the surrounding area. Return to Georgius to access 3 of the 4 pyramids.
2. Each activated pyramid opens a corresponding door inside the Shellcoof.
3. Explore the interior, collecting items and solving minor puzzles as you go.
4. Reach the Shellcoof's core to trigger the next story event.

---

#### Stage 9: Imperial Air Force Garrison

1. After the Shellcoof events, a battle against the Imperial Air Force Garrison is triggered.
2. Prepare for a series of consecutive fights against Imperial soldiers and mech weapons.
3. Focus on gauge management and don't hesitate to use healing items.

---

### DISC 3

---

#### Stage 10: Village of Zoah (Revisit) and Expanded Exploration

Disc 3 significantly expands your range of exploration.

1. Return to Zoah and check for new dialogue and events — many NPCs have updated conversations.
2. Receive new information and objectives from Paet.
3. Explore the **Forest of Zoah** — a plant-filled area on the outskirts. Lock on to and investigate objects to find rare items such as **Viratsuya Fruit**.

---

#### Stage 11: Uru Underground Ruins

A crucial area connected to the origins of the dragon.

1. Ride the dragon into the underground ruins.
2. On B4F and B2F North, locate **D-Units** — powerful dragon enhancement parts. Do not miss these.
3. Solve switch puzzles and navigate through each floor to reach the deepest level.
4. Areas previously accessible only by Floater (small airship) may now have new sections reachable by dragon.

**Tip:** D-Units dramatically boost the dragon's stats. Collect every one you find.

---

#### Stage 12: Mel-Kava

An Ancient defense weapon known as the "Chariot of God."

1. Approaching Mel-Kava triggers battle.
2. Focus your attacks on the **Reactors** (the wing-like structures keeping the vessel afloat). Destroy both reactors.
3. After Mel-Kava falls, face the **Boss: Atolm (3rd encounter)**.

**Atolm Strategy:**
- Apply everything you've learned about positioning — stay in safe zones and strike during openings.
- Use Berserk skills aggressively for heavy damage.

4. After defeating Atolm, the dragon evolves into the **Eye Wing** form. Laser Rank increases to #4.

---

### DISC 4

---

#### Stage 13: Final Exploration and Side Events

Before entering the Tower, tie up loose ends.

1. Revisit Zoah and Garil Desert to collect any missed items.
2. Re-explore the Forest of Zoah — new items appear after the events of Disc 3.
3. Optimize your dragon's morph settings. For the final battles, either a well-balanced build or the **Spiritual Class** (for powerful Berserks) is recommended.
4. Stock up heavily on healing items — you will need them.

---

#### Stage 14: The Tower

The final destination. A massive structure built by the Ancient civilization.

1. Fight through Imperial forces in the Tower's exterior to gain entry.
2. The Tower has multiple floors, each with gate switches and rotor pile control systems.
3. Destroy **Break Targets** (destructible objects) to collect items.
4. Battle powerful enemies such as **Scorpitara** and **Sentinels** as you ascend.
5. Descend to 1F North and follow the path to find the entrance to **Sestren**.

**Tower Tips:**
- Save at every save point you find — there is no going back.
- Use healing items freely to keep HP and BP high at all times.
- Some floor switches must be activated in the correct order.

---

#### Stage 15: Sestren — The Anti-Dragon Gauntlet

Sestren is the Ancient network that governs the world. Here, you face five **Anti-Dragons** in consecutive battles.

**Anti-Basic Wing**
- Straightforward attack patterns. Deal steady damage with Shots and Lasers.

**Anti-Valiant Wing**
- This enemy morphs between forms. Match your own dragon's morph to exploit its current weakness.

**Anti-Stripe Wing**
- Avoid danger zones while attacking with Shots from safe positions.

**Anti-Panzer Wing**
- A heavily armored opponent. Use Shield-type Berserks and defense-oriented tactics to outlast it.

**Anti-Eye Wing**
- Endure in safe zones and strike during brief openings.

**Tip:** Each Anti-Dragon requires a different strategy. Watch your healing item supply and BP reserves carefully between fights.

---

#### Stage 16: Final Boss — Sestren

**Phase 1: The Orbs**
1. Multiple orbs appear in the arena.
2. Destroy each orb one by one.
3. Destroy the final orb with all three gauges full — you'll carry two gauges into the next phase.

**Phase 2: The Solo Wing**
1. The weak point is the spectral Solo Wing flying around the arena.
2. Move to the rear position and charge all three gauges.
3. Move to the front and unleash concentrated Shots (gun fire).
4. After a while, Sestren changes its pattern and summons a **Ghost Dragon**.
5. While the Ghost Dragon is present, retreat to a safe zone and defend.
6. When the pattern reverts, resume attacking.

**Phase 3: The Final Push**
1. All zones start as safe (green), but turn red (dangerous) one by one.
2. Stay in green zones as long as possible.
3. Once all zones are red, Sestren summons phantoms of past bosses and unleashes devastating attacks.
4. At this point, go all-in — spam **Berserker Rage** and every powerful Berserk you have.
5. Pour all remaining resources into damage and defeat Sestren.

---

#### Ending

After Sestren is destroyed, the Heresy Program speaks to Edge, revealing that the will of the Ancients was entrusted to this program alone, and that the Sestren AI has been completely annihilated. It also reveals that the dragon was not the divine savior that Gash and the Seekers believed it to be — its purpose was more nuanced and bittersweet.

The ending resolves the stories of Edge and Azel, and a **Play Result** screen appears, evaluating your playthrough based on exploration rate, clear time, and other criteria.

## Chapter 5: Tips for First-Time Players

**Save often.** Always save at every save point. This is especially critical before boss fights — there are no checkpoints mid-battle.

**Master positioning.** The difference between a smooth victory and a crushing defeat often comes down to where you are on the radar. Always check the safe zones (green) and danger zones (red) before committing to an attack.

**Use dragon morphing strategically.** Different bosses call for different dragon classes. Use Agility Class for random encounters (faster gauge charging = quicker battles), and switch to Spiritual Class or Defense Class for boss fights.

**Talk to NPCs repeatedly.** In Zoah especially, talking to the same NPC multiple times can unlock new dialogue, items, and side events. Check both day and night.

**Don't hoard items.** Healing items are there to be used. Stock up whenever you can buy them, and use them freely in tough fights. Running out of HP in a boss battle because you were "saving" a potion is a common mistake.

**Explore every corner.** Hidden passages behind waterfalls, secret areas behind breakable targets, and tucked-away items reward thorough exploration.

**Learn Berserks in different forms.** The dragon learns different Berserk skills depending on its current morph configuration. Spend time in various forms during regular battles to expand your repertoire before the endgame.
